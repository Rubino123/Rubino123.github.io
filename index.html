About the Book

Osker’s curiosity leads him into the deep ocean, where he meets new creatures, faces surprising challenges, and discovers bravery he never knew he had. With watercolor illustrations and gentle humor, this story brings young readers into a world of shimmering blues, friendly sea life, and heartfelt lessons.

Inside the Story

From a giant shark who needs help to a glowing pearl that saves the day, Osker’s journey is filled with moments that blend fear, fun, and friendship. A perfect read‑aloud for bedtime or classroom story time.

About the Author

Raymond H. Johnson, known to young readers as “Dr. Ray at Play,” brings imagination, warmth, and gentle humor to every story he writes. His work inspires courage, curiosity, and kindness — themes at the heart of Osker the Oyster’s Great Adventure.

With a lifelong passion for storytelling and education, Dr. Ray creates playful, meaningful adventures that help children explore emotions and discover bravery in unexpected places.
